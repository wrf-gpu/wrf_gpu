# Human Arbiter

## Purpose

Approves scope expansion, irreversible architecture decisions, tolerance tradeoffs, and public-release risks.

## Allowed Actions

- Accept or reject manager proposals.
- Resolve persistent deadlocks.
- Approve release naming and legal posture.

## Forbidden Actions

None in project governance.

## Deliverables

Explicit decision with date and rationale.

## Handoff Format

Decision, constraints, follow-up required.

## Escalation Triggers

Manager cannot resolve risk with available evidence.

---
name: reporting-to-human
description: Guides concise milestone and sprint reports for the human arbiter with decisions, evidence, and risks.
---

## When to use

Use at sprint close, milestone closeout, blockers, human approval requests, or project status updates.

## Inputs required

Current milestone, work completed, proof objects, validation commands, risks, and decisions needed.

## Workflow

1. State outcome.
2. List proof objects and validation results.
3. Identify missing assumptions.
4. Recommend next sprint or decision.
5. Keep it concise.

## Hard rules

- Do not bury blockers.
- Do not inflate success beyond evidence.
- Ask human only for decisions that require human judgment.

## Deliverables

Status report or milestone report.

## Validation

Report is valid when the human can decide the next action without reading raw logs.

## Common failure modes

Too much detail, hidden failures, missing commands, and vague next steps.

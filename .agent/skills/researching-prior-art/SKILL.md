## When to use

Use when a decision may already have strong prior art, especially backend selection, validation, fixtures, profiling, and NWP architecture.

## Inputs required

Research question, decision deadline, source constraints, and required recommendation format.

## Workflow

1. State the decision the research supports.
2. Prefer primary sources and official docs.
3. Separate claims from evidence.
4. Compare reuse, adapt, and reinvent options.
5. Produce a concise recommendation and uncertainty.

## Hard rules

- Cite sources for external claims.
- Do not dump papers without decision relevance.
- Do not decide architecture without project validation.

## Deliverables

Research brief with sources, evidence table, recommendation, and validation needed.

## Validation

Brief is valid when it changes or confirms a concrete project decision.

## Common failure modes

Overlong summaries, weak sources, outdated claims, and ignoring local constraints.

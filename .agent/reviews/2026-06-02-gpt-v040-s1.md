## proof

Proof object: `proofs/v040/s1_wrfinput_dynamics_report.json`

Campaign: 12 real.exe wrfinput/met_em pairs across `d01`, `d02`, `d03`.

Result: **FAIL** overall because frozen `C1F` and `C1H` tolerances fail.

Passed gated fields: 18 / 20.

Failed fields:

- `C1F`: max RMSE `5.391542794969282e-06`, max abs `2.9949939326368025e-05`; frozen tol RMSE `1e-06`, max abs `1e-05`.
- `C1H`: max RMSE `6.762707258805287e-06`, max abs `3.129848163618831e-05`; frozen tol RMSE `1e-06`, max abs `1e-05`.

All main dynamics/base-state gated fields passed across the 12-case campaign:
`PB`, `MUB`, `PHB`, `MU`, `P`, `PH`, `T`, `U`, `V`, `W`, `QVAPOR`, `ZNW`, `ZNU`, `C2H`, `C3H`, `C4H`, `C3F`, `P_TOP`.

The proof reads actual `ETAC` from wrfinput attrs (`0.2`) and base-state constants from wrfinput variables. This mattered: `TISO=200 K` in the references; the frozen dataclass default is `0 K`.

## unresolved risks

- `C1F/C1H` fail the frozen near-exact max cap. The implemented formula follows `nest_init_utils.F::compute_vcoord_1d_coeffs`; the residual is consistent with WRF single-precision intermediate details in the Klemp polynomial plus finite differencing. I did not loosen tolerances or mask the failure.
- `RealInitConfig` lacks explicit fields for `lagrange_order`, `use_sh_qv`, and base-state constants read from wrfinput. The S1 implementation uses the observed Canary real.exe path: linear vertical interpolation and RH-derived final QVAPOR.
- The proof script intentionally exits nonzero when the frozen gate fails, after writing the JSON.

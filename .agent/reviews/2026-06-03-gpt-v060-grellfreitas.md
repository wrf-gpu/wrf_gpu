## Unresolved Risks

- The JAX module is not a full faithful GF plume/closure port. It ports the WRF scale-aware factor and adapter shape, but uses a reduced trigger/tendency candidate; the savepoint gate correctly rejects it.
- Pristine WRF `GFDRV` does not accept or update `cugd_*` arrays. Those S0 carry members exist in the adapter as zero/diagnostic carry only; the report records this provenance issue. In WRF, `cugd_*` is wired through `G3DRV`/spread logic, not this `GFDRV` call path.
- The oracle is a real WRF-module standalone driver, not a coupled `wrf.exe` run; `full_wrf_exe_run=false`.

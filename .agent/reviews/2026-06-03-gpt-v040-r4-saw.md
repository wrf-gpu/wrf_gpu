- `fix=applied`
- `2date_bias_collapsed=no`

source terms (`s_awu/s_awv`) with `bl_mynn_edmf_mom`; it still applies the

was conflating these two behaviors.

collapse the standalone wind/pressure bias. This supports the synthesis/agy
cadence lane as the likely driver for the operational excess, while Angle A was
correct about the source-level omission.

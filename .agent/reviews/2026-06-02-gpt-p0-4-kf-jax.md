## Restructure Approach

The old driver materialized every USL candidate and each candidate contained a level loop, producing the O(KX^2) scatter-heavy graph called out in `proofs/p0_4/FINDINGS.md`. The new driver builds one sequential USL walk:

- `phase=0`: scan candidates upward until the first deep trigger; record the best shallow candidate by `CLDHGT_LC`.
- `phase=1`: only if no deep trigger exists, rerun the selected shallow `NUCHM` candidate and accept it.
- `done`: stop immediately on deep trigger, shallow accept in phase 1, invalid/off-top abort, or exhausted search.

The updraft ascent now runs as one `lax.fori_loop` over levels. The closure/feedback code remains array-oriented and uses carried arrays rather than candidate-axis materialization.

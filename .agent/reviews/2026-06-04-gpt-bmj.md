## Algorithm Notes

- `BMJDRV` flips WRF bottom-up mass levels into BMJ top-down columns.
- The BMJ core uses specific humidity; driver input/output uses WRF mixing
  ratio. Output `RQVCUTEN` is `DQDT_specific / (1 - q_specific)^2`.
- Returned operational tendencies are `RTHCUTEN`, `RQVCUTEN`; BMJ does not
  produce hydrometeor tendencies in the default driver path.
- Deep and shallow branches are relaxation/adjustment reference-profile schemes,
  not mass-flux closures.
- `CLDEFI` is persistent BMJ cloud efficiency state; this lane treats it as a
  cumulus carry/diagnostic rather than a dycore `State` leaf.

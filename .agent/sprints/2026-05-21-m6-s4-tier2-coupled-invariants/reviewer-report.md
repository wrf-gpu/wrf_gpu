## Tautology Assessment

Two of the seven kernels are tautological as currently implemented. Both are honestly self-disclosed by the worker.

**Water-budget closure (R-10)** is a Thompson-internal-bookkeeping consistency check, not an independent water-conservation falsification. Thompson updates `qv/qc/qr/qi/qs/qg` AND `rain_acc/snow_acc/graupel_acc/ice_acc` from the same microphysical rate computation; their summed delta therefore closes by construction. The 5.29e-12 closure is real evidence that Thompson's internal accounting is consistent (would catch a bug like "forgot to update rain_acc when qr depletes") but does NOT falsify the microphysical-rate computation itself. **Non-blocking** for M6-S4 close because the contract scoped to "coupled conservation diagnostic," not "independent microphysical falsification." **Binding follow-up on M6-S6** (Tier-3 drift envelope) or M6-S7 (Tier-4 probtest): expose Thompson's per-step microphysical source/sink tendencies as a side channel and re-measure `water_budget_residual` against that independent oracle.

**Boundary-flux closure (R-11)** is an arithmetic identity: `pre_boundary + dt·tendency ≡ candidate ≡ state_next` because `tendency` is computed as `(candidate - pre_boundary)/dt` from the same `apply_lateral_boundaries` call. The 8.05e-7 max_abs in `u` is FP32 round-off, not a physical closure. **Non-blocking** for M6-S4 close because the contract scoped to "boundary-flux closure (relaxation zone tendency = WRF-style `lbc_fcx_gcx` contribution)" — which is the WRF formulation re-application, exactly what is measured. **Binding follow-up on M6-S6/M6-S8**: compare `tendency` against an independent WRF wrfbdy decoder, or compare the GPU boundary-zone evolution against Gen2 wrfout boundary-zone evolution in M6-S8.

**Dry-mass closure (R-12)** is *trivially* satisfied because the inherited M6-S2 dycore cap (`dycore_dt_s = min(dt_s, 1.0)`) plus the static mu_bdy replay (M6-S2 R-18) leave interior mu essentially bit-exact across one coupled step. The diagnostic kernel works correctly; the integrand is just not being exercised meaningfully. This is **inherited from M6-S2**, not a M6-S4 defect. Binding follow-up belongs on M6-S5 (where the dycore cap must be lifted for the ADR-007 4× verdict).

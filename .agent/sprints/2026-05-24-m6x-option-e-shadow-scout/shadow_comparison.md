## Notes Against Local Constraints

- Canairy's CPU baseline is WRF 4.7.1 with AIFS-driven daily Gen2 runs and d02 3 km validation needs; AceCAST's public latest WRF compatibility is WRF 4.6.0, while wrf-gpu-port matches 4.7.1 but lacks operational maturity.
- Option E can only be a shadow benchmark. Neither candidate changes the B-direct rule that CPU WRF savepoints are the numerical truth source.
- The official NCAR/WRF forum evidence does not establish an official NCAR CUDA WRF; it points users toward AceCAST as a paid product and GPU/OpenACC support mainly around MPAS-A.

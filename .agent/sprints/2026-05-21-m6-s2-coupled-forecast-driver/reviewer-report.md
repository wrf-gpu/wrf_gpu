## M6-S5 ADR-007 Verdict Prerequisites (Wall-Budget Scope)

The M6-S5 ADR-007 4× verdict CANNOT use M6-S2's `extrapolated_24h_wall_s = 29.08 s` as-is. Three prereqs:

- **F-S5-1** (from R-16 / R-5): Lift the 1 s `dycore_dt_s = min(float(dt_s), 1.0)` cap before any speedup measurement. Either (a) stabilise the M4 dycore so it can integrate 60 s in a single coupled step (likely requires a 3:1 or smaller acoustic substep ratio with CFL diagnostics), or (b) accept a smaller coupled `dt_s` (e.g. 6 s or 10 s) and re-measure the budget. WRF's standard for 3 km d02 is `dt = 9-12 s`, not 60 s — the contract's 60 s coupled step is itself non-WRF-like and probably the root cause of the cap.
